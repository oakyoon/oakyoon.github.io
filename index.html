Oakyoon Cha

Oakyoon Cha holds the position of assistant professor in the Department of Psychology at Sogang University. He studies visual awareness using cognitive neuroscience and computational modeling. For more details, visit his CV (https://oakyoon.net/cv/) and Google Scholar page. While he is not active on social media, you can find the latest updates from his lab on the DCANT Lab website (https://dcantlab.org).
